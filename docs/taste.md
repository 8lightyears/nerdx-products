# Owner taste

What the owner likes and rejects in product concepts and visual output. Agents
must read this before any visual work, and must append every owner rejection —
with its reason, as specifically as known — after it happens. Specific beats
general.

## Hard rules

- Every product must carry a **real, checkable idea** (science / history /
  curiosity), not story-less decoration.
- Equations, diagrams, and scientific claims must be **correct** — the owner has
  a physics background and will notice errors.
- Only use imagery with a **known, recorded license** (public domain pre-1927, or
  explicit CC/PD). Unknown license = unusable.

## Preferences

- Spirit: discovery / adventure — the shop and each product should feel like
  something is being uncovered. Working slogan: "Shirts that start interesting
  conversations."
- Favour clean, print-safe artwork at high resolution.
- A product can ship as a *setup*: the design plus a curated trail of links/
